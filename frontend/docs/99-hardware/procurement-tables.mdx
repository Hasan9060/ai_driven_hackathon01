### Procurement Best Practices

#### Volume Purchasing Considerations

1. **Educational Discounts**
   - NVIDIA: 10-20% educational pricing
   - Dell: Academic purchasing programs
   - CDW-G: Government and education contracts

2. **Bulk Ordering Strategies**
   - Order in quantities of 5+ for better pricing
   - Consider annual contracts for consumables
   - Bundle hardware with service contracts

3. **Warranty and Support**
   - 3-year warranties for critical infrastructure
   - Next business day on-site support for large deployments
   - Extended support for GPU components

4. **Total Cost of Ownership**
   - Factor in 5-year lifecycle costs
   - Include power consumption estimates
   - Budget for periodic upgrades and replacements

## Conclusion

The procurement tables and specifications provided above enable informed decision-making for establishing comprehensive Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics laboratories. Key considerations include:

1. **Balanced Performance**: Matching GPU, CPU, and memory capabilities to specific research needs
2. **Scalability**: Designing infrastructure that can grow with research requirements
3. **Reliability**: Selecting enterprise-grade components with appropriate support contracts
4. **Total Cost of Ownership**: Considering lifecycle costs beyond initial procurement
5. **Future-Proofing**: Investing in upgradeable components and standardized interfaces

These specifications represent current market conditions as of 2024 and should be regularly updated based on technological advances and market dynamics.
